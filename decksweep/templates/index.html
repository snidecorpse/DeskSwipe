Welcome to DeckSweep

Clean your folders like a game, with safety first.

How It Works

  1. Choose a folder root (Downloads by default).
  2. Swipe right to keep, left to queue for recycle bin.
  3. Tap folder cards to enter subdecks and sort inside.
  4. Review everything before any recycle-bin move.

Controls

  • Arrow Right: Keep
  • Arrow Left: Delete queue
  • Arrow Up: Enter folder
  • Space: Flip card

Safety & Privacy

  1. Purely local: data stays on your PC.
  2. No cloud upload: no folder contents are sent out.
  3. Recycle Bin only: delete actions move items to Recycle Bin.
  4. Manual final apply: nothing is moved until you confirm.
  5. Scoped scan: only the selected root folder is processed.
  6. Safer defaults: symlinks/junctions are skipped.

Always review queued items before apply if your files are sensitive.
